Graduation

December 16, 2016

After 6 long, yet lightning fast years, I have graduated. The expected length of the Computer Science program in my university is 9 semesters, but I took an extra 3 semesters because I wanted to do a lot of stuff, specially doing several internships. It was very much worth it, and all the experiences I had while at university helped shaping who I am today. Many people in university can’t wait until going out to the “real world”, only for them to look back and think about if only they had a bit more time. Like Elvis once said:

“Wise men say, only fools rush in”
